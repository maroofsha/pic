Who we are?

Hi there, We Are MaroofSha! We are a microscale handcrafting organization where individual strengths & skills are combined in the pursuit of a common vision. Our team members work as sales persons, marketers, dressmakers, web designers, distributors, retailers, soap makers, chefs, bloggers & photographers. Whatever we manufacture is handmade, from soaps, personal care products to food products.
